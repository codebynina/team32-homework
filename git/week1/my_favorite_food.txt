Vietnamese Pho Recipe

Ingredients:

1 lb beef bones
1 onion, halved
- 1-inch piece of ginger, sliced
- 5-6 star anise
- 1 cinnamon stick
- 3 cloves
- 2 tbsp fish sauce
- Rice noodles
- Fresh herbs (cilantro, basil)
- Bean sprouts
- Lime wedges
- Chili peppers (optional)

Instructions:
1. Roast the bones, onion, and ginger.
2. Boil the bones with water, add spices and fish sauce.
3. Simmer for several hours.
4. Cook noodles and assemble the pho with fresh herbs, sprouts, lime, and chili.
