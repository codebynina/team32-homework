Sushi Recipe

Ingredients:
- Sushi rice
- Nori (seaweed sheets)
- Fresh fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
- Soy sauce
- Wasabi
- Pickled ginger

Instructions:
1. Cook sushi rice and season with vinegar, sugar, and salt.
2. Place nori on a bamboo mat and spread rice evenly.
3. Add fish, roll the sushi, and cut into pieces.
4. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
